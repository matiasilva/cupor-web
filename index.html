Don't hesistate to reach out to us to express any concerns or to say hello!

Home away from home. Uma casa mais perto de ti.

CUPor brings the local Portuguese community as well as those interested in Portuguese culture together. It began humbly as three friends watching football together with Sagres, of course. We've grown and had some great times since!

Established in 2019, welcoming all since. Começámos em 2019, sempre de braços abertos.

Any member of the University of Cambridge, staff or student, can join us and come to one of our many events, even if you don't speak portuguese! We don't charge membership fees thanks to the generosity of our sponsors.
